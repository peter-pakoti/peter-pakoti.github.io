Emotional Intelligence can also be broken into three steps of 'Mindfulness', 'Self knowledge and Mastery', 'Creating Useful Mental Habits', which all play a massive roll Emotional Intelligence and with practice can lead you to a healthier state of mind and a healthier lifestyle overall and also develops habits to defefend against the superego (the critical, judging mind.)

What is Emotional Intelligence?

It is different in the way that IQ is more based around a persons ability to solve problems, use logic and grasp or communicate complex ideas where as EQ is based around your emotions and feelings within yourself and people around you.
